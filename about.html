Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals to take control of their health by providing them with a comprehensive platform to track, monitor, and manage their personal health and fitness data. Our startup focuses on leveraging technology and innovation to enhance the delivery, accessibility, and quality of healthcare services. We aim to address existing challenges in healthcare and offer solutions that improve patient care, outcomes, and overall well-being.

Our Vision

The vision of DQcare is to transform the way individuals manage their personal health and wellness, empowering them to lead healthier and more fulfilling lives. We envision a future where everyone has access to personalized health information, tools, and resources that enable them to make informed decisions about their well-being.

How We Started?

During the first COVID pandemic wave, Dr. Krunal (Founder) and Dr. Pooja (Co-Founder) were stranded on a Pacific Ocean island for almost 100 days. This experience led to the idea of a Digital Health Locker (DHL) to securely store patient and family health records online, known as an Electronic Health Record (EHR).

As doctors, they aimed to optimize patient care by creating a SMART digital health record (EHR) that saves time for both patients and doctors. They recognized the need for a user-friendly interface, which resulted in the birth of DQCare.
