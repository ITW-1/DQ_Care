Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals to take control of their health by providing them with a comprehensive platform to track, monitor, and manage their personal health and fitness data. Our startup focuses on leveraging technology and innovation to enhance the delivery, accessibility, and quality of healthcare services. We aim to address existing challenges in healthcare and offer solutions that improve patient care, outcomes, and overall well-being.

Our Vision

The vision of DQcare is to transform the way individuals manage their personal health and wellness, empowering them to lead healthier and more fulfilling lives. We envision a future where everyone has access to personalized health information, tools, and resources that enable them to make informed decisions about their well-being.

How we Started?

During the 1st wave of the COVID pandemic, Dr. Krunal (Founder) and Dr. Pooja (Co-Founder) were stranded on a Pacific Ocean island for almost 100 days. While they had the necessary documents to prove their identity, when it came to accessing medical facilities, they lacked the required information. This situation prompted them to conceive the idea of a unique Digital Health Locker (DHL) that would securely store all patient and family health records online, commonly known as an Electronic Health Record (EHR). Encouraged by others, they continued to explore ways to further simplify patients' lives.

As doctors, they realized that patients expected them to carefully review their physical files, listen attentively, and provide appropriate treatment. While this was their responsibility, they thought about optimizing this process in terms of time, cost, and care through a SMART digital health record (EHR). They envisioned a system that would save time for both patients and doctors, allowing the latter to quickly review all the necessary details at a glance.

They also recognized that having patient data on a device alone was insufficient. The Smart EHR would only be effective if doctors found it easy to navigate and utilize. This realization led to the meticulous organization and optimization of health details, ensuring convenience and usability for doctors at all times.

Thus, DQCare was born, designed with a unique formulation of health information that considers the perspectives of both patients and doctors.
